      Patterns

      A list of all the patterns I've discovered, as well as what they do.

      JSON Patterns

      These patterns were taken from a series of notes scrawled on the walls of the room of a Dougles Crockford shortly before their disappearence.

      They describe ways of creating and manipulating a dictionary-like object which uses Strings for keys and a select range of iotas as values. I wonder what the practical applications are...


      Jason's Reflection (→ json)

      • Renders
        • Palettes
          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edade

          Creates an empty JSON object.


          Jason's Purification (json, str → bool)

          • Renders
            • Palettes
              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edadee

              Checks whether the given JSON iota has the given key.


              Jason's Purification II (json, str → any)

              • Renders
                • Palettes
                  Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edadeedd

                  Gets the stored iota at the given key. Mishaps upon their being no iota.


                  Jason's Exaltation (json, str, any → json)

                  • Renders
                    • Palettes
                      Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: edadedaa

                      Sets the given iota at the given key.


                      Connection Patterns

                      Have you ever gotten to a point where, when searching for something, after checking the most obvious places, you then start searching the most obscure, random places you can find.

                      These patterns are the epitome of that. A search for parallel universes and the rips in the seams of Nature's domain. A guestimate if you will of where a rip will be.


                      Sturgis' Exaltation (num, num, num, num, num → conn)

                      • Renders
                        • Palettes
                          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qqqqwwwaqawwwqqqqwwwaqaww

                          Creates a new Connection given the four quartets of an IPv4 address and a port.


                          Sturgis' Distillation (str, num → conn)

                          • Renders
                            • Palettes
                              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qqqqwwewwqqqqwwwaqaww

                              Creates a new Connection given an address as a string and a port.


                              Sturgis' Distillation (→ conn)

                              • Renders
                                • Palettes
                                  Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qqqqwwewwqqqqwwew

                                  Creates a new Connection which points to ones own universe (127.0.0.1:80).


                                  Mitchell's Decomposition (conn → str, num)

                                  • Renders
                                    • Palettes
                                      Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qqqqweqqqqqd

                                      Breaks a Connection down into its address as a string and its port.


                                      Mitchell's Disintegration (conn → num, num, num, num, num)

                                      • Renders
                                        • Palettes
                                          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qqqqwwedew

                                          Breaks a Connection down into its address as its four quartets and its port.


                                          Isreal's Exaltation (conn, num, num, num, num → conn)

                                          • Renders
                                            • Palettes
                                              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qqqqwqeeeeew

                                              Changes a Connection's address to one made of the four provided quartets.


                                              Isreal's Distillation (conn, str → conn)

                                              • Renders
                                                • Palettes
                                                  Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qqqqweqaqe

                                                  Stanford's Distillation (conn, num → conn)

                                                      HTTP Spells

                                                      While pondering the depths of one's mind, Sir Bernes-Lee caught a glimpse into another universe. With this view fresh in his mind, he quickly conjured a JSON object, imbued it with information, and sent it hurdling into the other universe.

                                                      These are the patterns recovered after Berners-Lee was found dead having recieved enough information back to fry his mind.


                                                      Request (conn, json, str, str → res)

                                                      • Renders
                                                        • Palettes
                                                          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qqqqwqdqddqe

                                                          Sends an HTTP request made of the given data and method to the provided connection's endpoint.

                                                            • Palettes
                                                              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qqqqwweaaead
